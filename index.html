About HarmonyHub

Keightly is a software developer with a passion for music. Whether it's tackling a difficult coding problem or finishing a school assignment, music is a constant companion for Keightly. With a love for discovering new artists and songs, Keightly has always been curious about how music can connect people with similar interests.

It was this curiosity that led Keightly to develop an application that could provide better music recommendations and connect users with similar music tastes. With a focus on personalization, Keightly's application uses a variety of data points to suggest playlists and artists based on a user's listening history and preferences.

As a music lover and developer, Keightly is dedicated to creating an application that helps users discover new music and connect with others who share their passion.

This project is a Portfolio Project for Holberton School.
