About TuneBuddy

TuneBuddy is a music app designed to help you discover new music based on your personal preferences. With TuneBuddy, you can connect with like-minded music enthusiasts and get recommendations tailored just for you. Whether you're a die-hard fan or just starting out, TuneBuddy is the perfect companion for your musical journey.

Features

Personalized Recommendations

TuneBuddy uses advanced algorithms to analyze your listening habits and suggest music that fits your preferences.

Discover New Music

Explore new genres and artists, and connect with other TuneBuddy users who share your taste in music.

Real-Time Chat

Chat with other music lovers in real-time and share your favorite songs, artists, and playlists.
